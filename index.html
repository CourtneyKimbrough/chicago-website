Popular Attractions

Officially named Cloud Gate, the Chicago Bean is one of the city's most iconic landmarks.

From the iconic Centennial Wheel to waterfront dining, boat cruises, and seasonal events, there’s always something exciting happening at Navy Pier.

Whether you're soaking up the sun, playing volleyball, or biking along the Lakefront Trail, North Ave Beach the perfect place to relax and enjoy summer in the city!

The Chicago Riverwalk is a scenic waterfront promenade stretching through the heart of downtown. Lined with restaurants, bars, and public art, it’s the perfect spot for a stroll, a boat tour, or a sunset drink.

City of immigrants or city of heartless plutocrats, say what you will, Chicago almost defies interpretation.
